FAQ

Common Questions

How far do you travel for delivery? ▼
We're based in Bridgeport, CT and cover all of Fairfield County. Delivery within 30 miles is included free of charge. Beyond that, we charge $5 per mile. Just include your location in the quote request and we'll calculate it for you!
Is setup really included? ▼
100%! We deliver, fully set up every item, and return to break it all down and pick it up when the party's over. You don't need to lift a finger. Just point us to your space!
What do I need to provide for the bounce houses? ▼
You'll need a flat outdoor space (grass is ideal) and access to a standard outdoor electrical outlet within approximately 50 feet. For the bounce house with the mini pool, access to a garden hose/water is also needed. We bring everything else!
How does the cotton candy and popcorn pricing work? ▼
Our base pricing for both the cotton candy machine and popcorn machine covers up to 35 guests. If your party has more than 35 people, we add a per-person charge for the additional guests. Just let us know your headcount when you book and we'll factor it in your quote.
Do I need to pay a deposit? ▼
Yes — a deposit is required to officially reserve your date and lock in your rentals. The deposit amount will be outlined in your custom quote. It goes toward your total balance. The remaining amount is due on the day of the event.
Are the bounce houses safe? ▼
Absolutely. All of our inflatables are commercial-grade, regularly cleaned and inspected. We secure them properly during setup. We do ask that adult supervision is maintained at all times during use, and that children follow the basic safety rules we'll walk you through on arrival.
What if it rains on my party day? ▼
We monitor weather closely and will be in touch with you ahead of your event. Bounce houses should not be used in rain or high winds for safety. We'll work with you on rescheduling or adjusting your rental if weather becomes an issue. That's why adding a tent is a great idea!
How do I pay? ▼
We accept Cash, Zelle, and Venmo. The deposit can be sent upon booking confirmation, and the balance is due when we arrive on party day.
