Our Story

Built by the community,
for the community

Fun 360 was born right here in Fairfield County, CT — founded by people who believe every family deserves an amazing party without breaking the bank or doing all the work themselves.

Who We Are

We bring the fun to you

We saw a simple problem: families in Norwalk and Bridgeport were spending too much money and too much effort trying to put together a great party. Renting from companies far away, setting everything up themselves, and stressing out on a day that should be all about celebration.

Fun 360 changes that. We show up with everything, we set it all up, and we take it all down. All you have to do is show up and enjoy your family's big day.

We serve Norwalk, Bridgeport, Stamford, Trumbull, Fairfield, and all of Fairfield County, CT.
